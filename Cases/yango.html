How to grow an already successful business in a local market when you are already a market leader? There are two ways: the first is to increase frequency, thereby boosting DAU and monetization; the second is to expand into new markets.

In 2023, I was responsible for the interface side of the Yango Maps launch, and I will explain how we developed our product for a new market.

Certainly, the product must support the language of the country it is entering. We implemented RTL support, which we had been wanting to do for a long time.

Let's analyze how people perceive our product and understand its features. At the same time, we'll refine the wording and translations.
